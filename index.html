Hey! I'm Aditya, a data engineer who gets genuinely excited about turning messy data into systems that actually work. Based in Chicago, I spend my days building pipelines, optimizing platforms, and occasionally wondering why someone thought Excel was a good database.

I'm the person who can explain VPC endpoints to your non-technical PM, debug a Spark job at 2 AM (hopefully not often), and still find time to mentor junior engineers. My superpower? Making complex data infrastructure feel less scary and more like solving a really satisfying puzzle.
